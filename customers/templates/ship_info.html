Delivery Options

Shipping And Delivery

We work with reliable logistics partners to help your orders get to you safely, quickly, and with better delivery coverage.

GIG Logistics

GIG Logistics is one of Africa’s leading indigenous logistics companies, known for technology-powered delivery and strong support for e-commerce businesses. Through this delivery option, Modest Daily Fashion can serve customers across Nigeria and beyond with safer, faster, and more organized shipping.

DHL Logistics

DHL gives us access to a wider delivery network for local and international orders. With reliable tracking, express delivery options, and strong customs experience, DHL helps make long-distance shipping smoother and more transparent for customers.

Transport Riders

For faster local movement where suitable, Modest Daily Fashion may also use trusted transport riders. This helps us support quicker delivery within reachable locations when rider delivery is the best available option.

Note: Modest Daily Fashion will always choose the most suitable and fastest delivery method for your order based on your location, product availability, and the best logistics option at that moment.
